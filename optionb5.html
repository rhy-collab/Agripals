A word on how we work

We started Providores for a simple reason: the distance between a good farm and a good kitchen had filled up with people who added cost and little else. So we cut it back to the two ends that matter. We buy from growers and fishermen we can name, agree a fair price, and carry it ourselves to the chefs who’ll cook it.

The rest is discipline. Everything is graded before it leaves us and traceable back to where it came from. If it isn’t good enough for our own table, it doesn’t go on the truck. That is the whole of our philosophy, and it is the reason chefs stay.
