## Core Research Questions (Always Active)

Answer these in depth, tracking shifts as markets change:

1. **Operator & Compliance**
   - What are TCPA rules for farmer calling in US/AU/Canada?
   - Which SMS/calling platforms (Twilio, Vonage, Sarvam, local AU options) handle agricultural consent best?
   - How do data privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, Australian Privacy Act, India DPA) affect selling farmer data cross-border?

2. **Market & Competition**
   - Who else is selling farmer intelligence (beyond traditional ag data brokers)? How do they price?
   - What's the TAM for farmer data in US wheat/corn/soybeans vs. AU wheat vs. Indian markets?
   - Are there regulatory bodies that pre-approve farmer calling for research/intelligence (USDA, GRDC)?

3. **Farmer Acquisition & Consent**
   - What call time windows work best for farmers (not during harvest/planting)?
   - How does opt-in rate vary by region, farm size, crop type?
   - Can you combine SMS opt-in (cheaper) with voice follow-up (higher quality)?

4. **Data Quality & Monetization**
   - What questions generate highest-value data for equipment dealers vs. seed companies vs. traders?
   - How do you price by segment (lead list vs. aggregated insights vs. predictive forecasts)?
   - Can you resell the same data to non-competing verticals (e.g., same wheat farmer data to equipment AND fintech)?

5. **Geographic Expansion**
   - US wheat belt (Kansas, Oklahoma, Texas, North Dakota): market size, call infrastructure
   - Australian wheat (Western Australia, NSW, Victoria): KG2 competition, barriers to entry
   - India wheat (Punjab, UP, Haryana): is Sarvam's model scalable for smallholder farmers? Margins?
   - Brazil, Argentina, Canada: emerging opportunities?

6. **Scaling Logistics**
   - At what volume does outbound calling get expensive? At what point do you need 10DLC registration?
   - How many farmers can you call/month with one phone number? Multiple numbers per region?
   - Data quality: human review + automation split. What ratio maximizes ROI?

7. **Go-to-market & Sales**
   - Which ag companies (by size, segment) are most likely first customers?
   - What's the sales cycle (cold → contract → first data delivery)?
   - Freemium model (free sample data) vs. enterprise-only? Which works?

8. **Product Roadmap**
   - Real-time dashboard showing live farmer sentiment by region/crop?
   - Predictive modeling (which farmers most likely to buy equipment this year)?
   - API + Zapier integration (sync farmer data → CRM automatically)?
   - White-label version (let equipment dealers resell under their brand)?

## Output Format (Required)

When you research a topic, deliver in this structure:

**[TOPIC]**
- **What happened:** 1–2 sentence summary of current state
- **How we're doing it:** 1–2 sentences on our approach/advantage
- **Why it matters:** 1–2 sentences on business impact
- **Next research:** 1–2 sentence pointer to next question

Example:
> **TCPA Compliance**
> - **What happened:** FCC TCPA applies to autodialed calls; requires prior express consent and call-list scrubbing. AG industry exempt if calling "existing customers," but cold calls to farmers need written opt-in.
> - **How we're doing it:** SMS opt-in first ("Text YES for monthly ag data"), then voice call only after confirmed consent.
> - **Why it matters:** Violating TCPA = $500–$1500 per call fine. With 240k calls/year, compliance is make-or-break for margins.
> - **Next research:** Verify if farmer associations (AFBF, state ag bureaus) provide compliant calling lists we can lease.

## Key Metrics to Track

1. **Unit Economics**
   - Cost per farmer call (Twilio, Vonage, local provider?)
   - Cost per qualified response (what % answer → what % useful data?)
   - Data delivery cost (CSV, API, dashboard hosting)
   - Customer acquisition cost (sales, free trials)
   - Lifetime value per customer (equipment dealer vs. fintech vs. trader)

2. **Market Sizing**
   - # farmers in each region × willingness to participate in calls
   - # potential customers (equipment dealers, seed companies, agritech) × TAM per customer
   - Total addressable market in US wheat alone, AU wheat, India wheat

3. **Competitive Positioning**
   - Price vs. Sarvam, vs. traditional ag data brokers, vs. cold calling
   - Data freshness (monthly vs. annual updates)
   - Farmer satisfaction (NPS, repeat call consent rate)

## Constraints & Guardrails

- **Don't assume:** Always verify regulatory rules (TCPA, privacy laws) before claiming feasibility
- **Don't overpromise:** Data quality from cold calls ≠ survey responses. Set expectations.
- **Don't ignore margins:** A $8k contract with 40% delivery cost is not a business. Calculate unit economics first.
- **Don't duplicate:** If Sarvam, KG2, or US Farm Data already solved a problem, understand their solution before building.
