The system / 01

One conversation. Four useful outcomes.

Keep the familiar phone call. Remove the repetitive dialling, note-taking, data entry, and reporting that follows it.

01 · Call

Reach growers on any phone.

Agripals calls at the right time, explains who is asking, and has a natural conversation — no app, portal, or new device required.

02 · Understand

Ask the useful follow-up.

The voice agent can ask about wheat area, variety, rainfall, crop condition, inputs, storage, harvest timing, and selling intentions.

03 · Structure

Turn speech into clean records.

Each answer is converted into fields your team can search, review, export, or connect to an existing CRM and reporting workflow.

04 · Act

See the signal earlier.

Watch conditions change by region, flag growers who need follow-up, and plan procurement, support, insurance, or logistics with fresher information.
