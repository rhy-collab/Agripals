Where the numbers come from

We ask farmers. Every month. With their permission.

No scraping, no guessing, no satellite squinting at bins from orbit. A consented panel of farmers answers a 90-second automated conversation each month — and gets the full report free in return.

Consent-first panel

Every farmer opted in — by text, signup, or a real phone conversation. Fully TCPA-compliant, opt-out any time. The panel is the moat, and it's built on trust.

AI-assisted conversations

Automated calls and SMS do in 90 seconds what call centers charge a fortune for — which is why we can ask thousands of farmers monthly, not hundreds quarterly.

Benchmarked, not vibes

Every panel read is stratified and checked against USDA benchmarks when they publish. When we're early, you'll see it. When we're wrong, you'll see that too.

Fair questions

Asked and answered.

How is this different from USDA data?

USDA is the benchmark and we treat it that way — but it reports stocks four times a year, in aggregate, with a lag. We report monthly, by region, from named-panel farmer conversations. Use both; act on ours first.

Is the farmer outreach legal?

Yes, because consent comes first. Farmers join the panel themselves — by inbound text, web signup, or a live recruitment call — before any automated contact. Every message identifies itself, every farmer can leave with one word, and the whole operation is built to TCPA and carrier rules.

Why would farmers keep answering?

Because they're the other customer. Every panelist gets the full paid report free — what their neighbors are holding and when they plan to sell is worth more to a farmer than to anyone. Data for data.

How accurate is a new panel?

Honestly: it earns trust issue by issue. Every read is published against the next USDA benchmark so you can score us. We'd rather show you a track record than claim one.

Can we ask our own questions?

Desk plans get one custom question per monthly cycle; Enterprise runs whole custom waves. Same panel, same method, your questions.
