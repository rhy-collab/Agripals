My RFQs

What's an RFQ?

RFQ = Request for Quote. When you request an item — say Wild-Caught Prawns, 15 lbs/week — Agripals sends it out to every farmer and supplier on the platform. Whoever can supply it accepts the job and delivers to you for an agreed period. If a farmer can no longer supply, it automatically passes to the next best one — so you're never left short. The whole point: a fresher, better product straight from the source — at a farm-direct price, with full traceability to the farm it came from.
