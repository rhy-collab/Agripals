What's a Bid?

A bid is a request for an item — say Wild-Caught Prawns, 15 lbs/week — that goes out to every farm on the platform to compete for. Here's the good bit: restaurants near you can join your bid, and you can join theirs. The more kitchens on a bid, the bigger the order — and the better the price everyone gets.
